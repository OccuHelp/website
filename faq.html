What is OccuHelp?

OccuHelp is a comprehensive workplace safety and health management software platform designed to help organizations prevent injuries, reduce health risks, and improve occupational health outcomes. Our solution combines incident tracking, risk assessment, analytics, and preventive action planning in one integrated system.

What features does OccuHelp offer?

OccuHelp offers a wide range of features, including:

  • Incident tracking and reporting
  • Risk assessment tools
  • Preventive action planning
  • Data analytics and trend identification
  • Compliance management
  • Mobile data collection
  • Customizable dashboards
  • Integration with other business systems

Which industries does OccuHelp serve?

OccuHelp serves a wide range of industries, including but not limited to:

  • Manufacturing
  • Construction
  • Healthcare
  • Transportation and logistics
  • Energy and utilities
  • Retail
  • Hospitality
  • Education

Our platform is flexible and can be customized to meet the specific needs of different industries and organizations.

How much does OccuHelp cost?

OccuHelp offers several pricing tiers to accommodate organizations of different sizes and needs. Our pricing is based on factors such as the number of users, selected features, and implementation requirements.

We offer flexible subscription plans starting from $X per user per month. For detailed pricing information tailored to your organization's needs, please contact our sales team.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes, we offer a 30-day free trial that gives you access to all features of our platform. This allows you to evaluate how OccuHelp can benefit your organization before making a commitment.

To start your free trial, please contact us, and one of our representatives will help you get set up.

Are there any long-term contracts?

We offer both monthly and annual subscription options. Annual subscriptions come with a discount compared to monthly billing. While we don't require long-term contracts, many of our clients choose annual plans for budget predictability and cost savings.

How long does it take to implement OccuHelp?

Implementation time varies depending on the size of your organization, the complexity of your requirements, and the level of customization needed. Typically, basic implementation can be completed in 2-4 weeks, while more complex implementations may take 6-8 weeks.

Our implementation team works closely with you to establish a timeline and ensure a smooth transition to OccuHelp.
