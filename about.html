About OccuHelp

Founded in 2015, OccuHelp has been at the forefront of transforming how organizations approach workplace health and safety. Our innovative software solutions help businesses identify risks, prevent injuries, and manage occupational health more effectively.

Our Approach

At OccuHelp, we believe that workplace safety and employee health are not just regulatory requirements but essential components of a successful business. Our approach combines cutting-edge technology with evidence-based practices to create solutions that are:

  • Data-driven - Leveraging analytics to identify patterns and predict potential issues
  • User-friendly - Designed with the end-user in mind for maximum adoption
  • Customizable - Adaptable to the unique needs of different industries and organizations
  • Integrated - Seamlessly connecting with existing systems and workflows

Our Team

The OccuHelp team brings together expertise from occupational health, software development, data science, and business management. This interdisciplinary approach allows us to understand the complex challenges our clients face and develop solutions that address their specific needs.

Leadership

Our leadership team combines decades of experience in workplace health and safety with technical expertise in software development and data analytics.

Development

Our developers work closely with occupational health experts to create intuitive, powerful tools that make a real difference in workplace safety outcomes.
