Comprehensive Tools

From risk analysis to medical management and operations

Risk Analysis

Identify and assess workplace risks to prevent injuries before they occur

Our Risk Analysis tools help you identify potential hazards and assess their severity, allowing you to prioritize interventions and allocate resources effectively. From broad program evaluations to detailed task analyses, these tools provide a comprehensive framework for understanding and mitigating workplace risks.

Program Analysis

Evaluate the effectiveness of your overall safety program with comprehensive assessments that identify strengths and areas for improvement. Track progress over time and benchmark against industry standards.

Site Analysis

Assess specific work locations for potential hazards and risk factors. Document site conditions, identify environmental concerns, and develop site-specific safety protocols.

Job Analysis

Break down job roles to identify associated risks and physical demands. Create detailed job profiles that inform hiring practices, accommodation strategies, and injury prevention efforts.

Task Analysis

Examine specific work tasks to identify ergonomic risk factors and potential injury mechanisms. Develop targeted interventions to reduce risk at the task level.

Risk Management

Track identified risks, implement control measures, and monitor their effectiveness. Maintain a comprehensive risk register and ensure continuous improvement in risk reduction.

Key Benefits

  • Identify high-risk areas before injuries occur
  • Prioritize interventions based on risk severity
  • Document compliance with safety regulations
  • Track risk reduction progress over time
  • Generate comprehensive risk assessment reports

Prevention

Proactive tools to prevent injuries and promote workplace wellness

Ergonomics Assessment Tool

Key Benefits

  • Reduce workplace injuries through proactive measures
  • Improve employee health and wellbeing
  • Decrease workers' compensation costs
  • Increase productivity and reduce absenteeism
  • Create a culture of safety and prevention

Our Prevention tools focus on proactive measures to prevent injuries before they occur. From ergonomic assessments to wellness programs and pre-employment testing, these tools help you create a healthier, safer workplace environment and reduce the risk of injuries and illnesses.

Prehab

Implement preventive rehabilitation programs targeting common injury areas. Develop customized exercise protocols and track employee participation and outcomes.

Wellness

Create and manage comprehensive wellness initiatives that address physical, mental, and emotional health. Track participation, measure outcomes, and demonstrate ROI.

Ergonomics

Conduct detailed ergonomic assessments, identify risk factors, and implement targeted interventions. Document workstation adjustments and track improvements over time.

Stretching & Strengthening

Design job-specific stretching and strengthening programs. Create visual guides, schedule group sessions, and monitor compliance and effectiveness.

Physical Demands Analysis

Document the physical requirements of specific job roles. Quantify force, frequency, and duration of tasks to inform hiring, accommodation, and return-to-work decisions.

Employment Testing

Design and administer pre-employment and post-offer testing protocols. Ensure job candidates can safely perform essential functions and document testing procedures.

Medical

Comprehensive tools for managing workplace injuries and health conditions

Our Medical tools provide a complete system for managing workplace injuries and health conditions. From self-care guidance to comprehensive case management, these tools help you respond effectively to health issues, minimize their impact, and facilitate successful return to work.

Self Care

Provide employees with guided self-care protocols for minor discomfort. Document self-care attempts and track outcomes to identify recurring issues that may require additional intervention.

Early Intervention

Implement rapid response protocols for emerging symptoms before they become recordable injuries. Track interventions and their effectiveness to refine your early response strategies.

Injury or Illness

Maintain comprehensive electronic medical records for workplace injuries and illnesses. Document assessments, treatments, restrictions, and follow-up care in a secure, HIPAA-compliant system.

MSK Rehab

Manage musculoskeletal rehabilitation programs with specialized tools for tracking progress, documenting exercises, and monitoring outcomes. Integrate with return-to-work planning.

Case Management

Coordinate all aspects of complex cases with comprehensive case management tools. Track appointments, communications, restrictions, accommodations, and return-to-work progress.

Case Management Dashboard

Key Benefits

  • Maintain secure, HIPAA-compliant medical records
  • Streamline documentation and reporting
  • Improve case outcomes through consistent management
  • Facilitate effective return-to-work planning

Operations

Essential tools for managing safety operations and compliance

OSHA Recordkeeping Dashboard

Key Benefits

  • Ensure regulatory compliance with automated tools
  • Streamline incident reporting and investigation
  • Maintain accurate safety documentation
  • Track safety performance metrics

Our Operations tools help you manage the day-to-day aspects of workplace safety and compliance. From incident management to OSHA recordkeeping and inventory control, these tools streamline administrative tasks and ensure you meet regulatory requirements while maintaining efficient operations.

Incident Manager

Document, investigate, and track workplace incidents. Identify root causes, implement corrective actions, and generate comprehensive incident reports for internal and external stakeholders.

OSHA Recordkeeping

Maintain OSHA-compliant injury and illness records with automated tools for determining recordability. Generate OSHA 300, 300A, and 301 forms and track key safety metrics.

Safety Data Sheets

Manage your SDS library with tools for organizing, searching, and accessing safety data sheets. Ensure compliance with hazard communication standards and provide easy access to critical safety information.

Inventory

Track medical supplies, safety equipment, and other inventory items. Set reorder points, monitor usage patterns, and maintain appropriate stock levels to support your safety and health programs.

Quality Assurance

Implement quality control processes for your safety and health programs. Conduct audits, track performance metrics, and ensure consistent application of policies and procedures.

Multi-Company Support

Flexible solutions for diverse organizational structures

OccuHelp's platform is designed to accommodate various organizational structures with its flexible multi-company architecture. Whether you're a single organization, a parent company with subsidiaries, or a service provider managing multiple clients, our system adapts to your needs.

Single Organizations

For businesses using the software for their own operations, you'll typically use just one company profile. This streamlines your experience while still giving you access to all the powerful tools in the platform.

Parent Companies with Subsidiaries

If you oversee multiple subsidiary businesses, you can create a separate company profile for each entity. This allows you to maintain distinct data sets while still being able to generate consolidated reports and analytics across your entire organization.

Occupational Health Service Providers

For organizations that provide occupational health staffing services, our platform allows you to create a separate company profile for each client you serve. This ensures complete data separation while giving you efficient tools to manage all your clients from a single interface.
