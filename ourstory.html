Our Story

The journey of OccuHelp is one of innovation, dedication, and a relentless commitment to improving workplace safety. Here's how our story unfolded.

2015

The Beginning

OccuHelp was founded by a team of occupational health professionals and software engineers who recognized a critical gap in workplace safety technology. After witnessing the limitations of paper-based systems and disconnected software solutions, they set out to create an integrated platform that could truly transform how organizations approach workplace health and safety.

2016

First Product Launch

After months of development and testing, we launched our first product: a basic incident tracking and reporting system. While simple by today's standards, it laid the foundation for our data-driven approach to workplace safety management.

Our first clients were small manufacturing companies looking to improve their safety records and streamline compliance reporting. Their feedback was invaluable in shaping our product roadmap.

2017

Expanding Capabilities

Building on the success of our initial offering, we expanded our platform to include risk assessment tools and preventive action planning. This shift from reactive to proactive safety management resonated strongly with our growing client base.

"The moment we realized we could help prevent injuries before they happened, not just track them afterward—that's when we knew we were onto something truly transformative."

— OccuHelp Co-Founder

2018

Analytics Revolution

In 2018, we introduced our analytics engine, which allowed clients to identify patterns and trends in their safety data. This breakthrough feature enabled organizations to make more informed decisions about resource allocation and intervention strategies.

During this period, we also expanded our team, bringing on data scientists and UX specialists to enhance our platform's capabilities and usability.

2019

Industry Recognition

Our innovative approach to workplace safety management began to gain industry recognition. We were honored with several awards for innovation in occupational health technology and were featured in leading industry publications.

This recognition helped us expand into new markets and industries, from healthcare to construction and beyond.

2020

Adapting to Global Challenges

When the COVID-19 pandemic hit, we quickly pivoted to help our clients navigate new workplace health challenges. We developed modules for contact tracing, health screening, and remote work safety assessments.

This period reinforced our commitment to being responsive to our clients' evolving needs and demonstrated the flexibility of our platform.

2021-Present

Continued Growth and Innovation

In recent years, we've continued to expand our platform's capabilities, incorporating machine learning for predictive analytics and developing mobile applications for field use. We've also grown our team and client base significantly.

Today, OccuHelp serves organizations of all sizes across multiple industries, helping them create safer workplaces and protect their most valuable asset—their people.
